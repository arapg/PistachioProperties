About us

Here at Pistachio Properties we take pride in offering only the most efficient and wide reaching real estate services, offering our expertise as a brokerage to connect sellers with buyers from a wide demographic of clients with individualised listings and expert support. Our company has an influence in many regions, with contacts in multiple countries, making us an international presence that offers expert advice in immigration, taxation, and other administrative details when entering the housing market on a global stage.


The Team

Our team of experts is inclusive and diverse, with individuals trained in many institutions around the world, and are all fully accredited on our industry leading training program. Our sales representatives and immigration officers are all multitalented individuals who strive to help you match you to your dream home, or you to your ideal seller - we will work tirelessly to give you the best service possible. We are always happy to help and support you through any enquiries, feel free to reach out and contact us and our friendly team will get in touch!
